Front Matters

The Performance Benchmarking Working Group aims to foster the understanding of the ICAO Global Air Navigation Plan (GANP) key performance indicators and jointly works on establishing commonly agreed definitions and algorithms in support of the GANP. This report reflects a set of air traffic measures addressing the current COVID/post-COVID developments.

PBWG Members are

  • Brazil, DECEA
  • Europe, EUROCONTROL
  • Japan, JCAB
  • Singapore, CAAS
  • Thailand, AEROTHAI
  • United States of America, FAA
